第七篇 蒙憐憫得尊貴和榮耀的器皿、麥粒、和葡萄樹的枝子

六、 我們應當與運行的靈合作，並接受神為我們所安排的環境—腓四12，弗三1，四1，六20，林前七24。

D. A Christian who is spiritual for the building up of the Body of Christ must “read” three things every day—he needs to read the Bible, he needs to read his inward sense, and he needs to read his environment and circumstances, which are the people, things, and matters around him—Rom. 8:6; cf. Prov. 16:9.

七、 我們可能向神抱怨,但我們的抱怨可能是最好的禱告,神最喜悅的禱告;當我們在抱怨時,神在喜樂,因為他使萬有都互相効力,叫我們得益處,為使我們模成他長子的形像—參詩一○二標題。詩102 標題 困苦人發昏的時候,在耶和華面前傾吐苦情的禱告。

E. We may pray and hope that others will change, but the more we pray in this way, the clearer we are that nothing will change; this is the environment that God has created to cause us to be conformed to the image of God’s Son and manifest God’s grace and power—Rom. 8:28-29; cf. 6:3-4; 1 Kings 7:17, 20-22.

參、 在基督裏的信徒,乃是葡萄樹的枝子—約十五1,4~5:

I. would speak boldly, as I ought to speak.

一、 葡萄樹及其枝子,就是基督和在基督裏的信徒,是神經綸中三一神的生機體,因神的豐富而長大,並彰顯他神聖的生命—1~5 節。

G. We may complain to God, but our complaining may be the best prayer, the most pleasant prayer to God; while we are complaining, God is rejoicing because He is causing all things to work together for good that we may be conformed to the image of His firstborn Son—cf. Psa. 102, title. Psa. 102, title—A Prayer of an afﬂicted one, when he is fainting and pours out his complaint before Jehovah.

二、 離了基督這葡萄樹,我們就一無所是,一無所有,也一無所能;因此,我們需要住在祂裏面,就是停留、住留、並居住在他裏面;不憑我們的所是或所能,乃憑不死的生命—基督自己—而活—十四6 上,林後五4。

A. The vine, which is Christ, with its branches, which are the believers in Christ, is the organism of the Triune God in God’s economy to grow with His riches and to express His divine life—vv. 1-5.

B. We are nothing, we have nothing, and we can do nothing apart from Christ as the vine; thus, we need to abide in Him, to remain, stay, and dwell in Him, not living by what we are or by what we can do but by the immortal life, which is Christ Himself—14:6a; 2 Cor. 5:4.

    10. If you keep My commandments, you will abide in My love; even as I have kept My Father’s commandments and abide in His love.

D. To abide in Christ is to keep ourselves in the fellowship with Him daily and hourly, not allowing anything to come between us and Him; the entire church life depends on the divine fellowship, which is the circulating Triune God working, flowing, communicating, transporting, transmitting, and dispensing all that He is into us for our enjoyment—1 John 1:3; 2 Cor. 13:14.

五、 我們要讓主住在我們裏面，就必須讓祂的話住在我們裏面；我們若藉著到祂面前來得生命而住在祂所寫的話裏，祂即時的話就要住在我們裏面作靈和生命一約十五7，五39~40 ，八31，六63。
