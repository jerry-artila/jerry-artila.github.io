二〇二六年六月半年度訓練

第六篇 神長子基督的眾弟兄

Message Six: Brothers of Christ as the Firstborn Son of God

讀經：撒下七 12 上，14 上，羅一 3~4，八 28~30，來二 10~12，林前十五 31，36

壹、 『我必興起你…的後裔…，我要作他的父，他要作我的子』一撒下七12 上，14 上：

I. “I will raise up your seed…I will be his Father, and he will be My son”—2 Sam. 7:12a, 14a:

一、 這話符合保羅在羅馬一章三至四節的話，到基督是大衛的後裔，在復活裏，在祂的人性裏被標出為神的兒子。

A. This word corresponds with Paul’s in Romans 1:3-4 concerning Christ as the seed of David being designated the Son of God in His humanity in resurrection.

二、 撒下七章的內在意義，乃是三一神在祂經過過程並終極完成的三一裏，將他自己作到祂所揀選的人裏面。

B. The intrinsic significance of 2 Samuel 7 is that the Triune God is working Himself in His processed and consummated Trinity into His chosen people.

三、 按希伯來一章五節下半（那裏引用了撒下七章十四節上半），大衛的後裔就是基督作神的長子，兼有神性和人性一來一5 上，6。

C. According to Hebrews 1:5b, which quotes 2 Samuel 7:14a, David’s seed is Christ as God’s firstborn Son, who has both divinity and humanity—Heb. 1:5a, 6.

四、 撒下七章十二節上半『你…的後裔』和十四節上半『我的子』，含示大衛的後裔要成為神的兒子，也就是，人的後裔要成為神聖的子一羅一

D. Your seed in 2 Samuel 7:12a and My son in verse 14a imply that the seed of David would become the Son of God, that is, that a human seed would become a divine Son—Rom. 1:3-4.
  1. ~4 。
    4. Who was designated the Son of God in power according to the Spirit of holiness out of the resurrection of the dead, Jesus Christ our Lord;

五、 這含示神在祂經綸裏的心意，是要使他自己成為人，使人在生命和性情上，但不在神格上，成為神。

E. This implies that God’s intention in His economy is to make Himself man in order to make man God in life and nature but not in the Godhead.

六、 關於大衛的後裔成為神的兒子，這話在新約裏繼續且強有力的發展一太二二41~45， 二二16。

F. This word concerning the seed of David becoming the Son of God is continued and developed strongly in the New Testament—Matt. 22:41-45; Rev. 22:16.

貳、 在行傳十三章保羅說，從大衛的後裔中，『神已經照著所應許的，給以色列帶來一位救主，就是耶穌，』（23），並且『神已經……完全應驗［這應許］，叫上所記：「你是我的兒子，我今日生了你。」』（徒十三33）：

II. In Acts 13 Paul says that from David’s seed “God, according to promise, brought to Israel a Savior, Jesus” (v. 23), and that “God has fully fulﬁlled this promise…in raising up Jesus, as it is also written in the second Psalm, ‘You are My Son; this day have I begotten You’” (v. 33):

一、 在行傳十三章三十二至三十三節我們看見，基督作為神的長子，乃是神向祖宗的應許，並且神藉著叫耶穌復活，應驗這應許。

A. In verses 32 and 33 we see that Christ as the firstborn Son of God was promised to the fathers, and God fulfilled this promise by raising up Jesus.

二、 復活對那人耶穌乃是出生：

B. Resurrection was a birth to the man Jesus:
  1. 在復活裏，他被神生為許多弟兄中的長子—羅八29。
    1. He was begotten by God in His resurrection to be the firstborn Son of God among many brothers—Rom. 8:29.
  2. 祂從永遠就是神的獨生子—約一18，三16。
    2. He was the only begotten Son of God from eternity—John 1:18; 3:16.
  3. 成為肉體以後，祂藉著復活，在人性裏被神生為神的長子—來一6。
    3. After His incarnation, through resurrection He was begotten by God in His humanity to be God’s firstborn Son—Heb. 1:6.

三、 保羅能在詩篇二篇七節看見主的復活：『你是我的兒子，我今日生了你』：

C. Paul was able to see the Lord’s resurrection in Psalm 2:7: “You are My Son; / Today I have begotten You” :
  1. 保羅把『今日』一辭應用到主復活的日子。
    1. Paul applies the word today to the day of the Lord’s resurrection.
  2. 這就是說，基督的復活就是祂生為神的長子。
    2. This means that Christ’s resurrection was His birth as the firstborn Son of God.
  3. 人子耶穌藉著從死人中復活，生為神的兒子；所以，神使耶穌從死人中復活，乃是將祂生為神的長子—徒
    3. Jesus, the Son of Man, was born to be the Son of God through being raised from the dead; therefore, God’s raising up Jesus from the dead was His begetting of Him to be His firstborn Son—Acts 13:33.

十三、 33。

D. Through incarnation God’s only begotten Son put on humanity and became the God-man (John 1:14, 18; Luke 1:35); then in resurrection this God-man was born of God to be His Firstborn (Acts 13:33; Heb. 1:6; Rom. 8:29):
  1. 神的獨生子在成為肉體以前，沒有屬人的性情，只有神聖的性情。
    1. Before incarnation God’s only begotten Son did not have the human nature; He had only the divine nature.
  2. 神的長子在復活裏 ，有神聖的性情，也有屬人的性情。
    2. In resurrection God’s firstborn Son has the human nature as well as the divine nature.

五、 基督藉著祂的復活，生為神的長子，同時祂所有的信徒也生為神許多的兒子—彼前一3，來二10：

E. Through His resurrection Christ was born to be the firstborn Son, and at the same time all His believers were born to be the many sons of God—1 Pet. 1:3; Heb. 2:10:
  1. :10 原來萬有因祂而有，藉祂而造的那位，為著要領許多的兒子進榮耀裏去，就藉著苦難成全他們救恩的創始者，這對祂本是合宜的。
    1. Among these many sons, only the Firstborn is God’s only begotten Son—1 Pet. 1:3; Heb. 2:10.
  2. 在神許多兒子當中，只有長子是神的獨生子—彼前一3，來二10。
    2. This only begotten Son of God, in His resurrected humanity, is also the firstborn Son of God—Rom. 8:29.
  3. 這位神的獨生子在祂復活的人性裏，也是神的長子—羅八29。
    3. The Firstborn has both divinity and humanity, and we, His believers as God’s many sons, also possess both the human nature and the divine nature—2 Pet. 1:4.
  4. 神的長子兼有神性和人性，我們這些作神許多兒子的信徒，也兼具屬人的性情和神聖的性情—彼後一4 。
    1. By incarnation, Christ, the only begotten Son of God in His divinity (John 1:18), put on the flesh, the human nature, which had nothing to do with divinity; in His humanity, He was not the Son of God:
  5. 耶穌在祂的人性裏是大衛的後裔，是屬於神舊造（舊人—羅六6）的人性後裔—西一15 下。
    a. Jesus in His humanity was the seed of David, a human seed belonging to the old creation (the old man—Rom. 6:6) of God—Col. 1:15b.
  6. 當基督在祂的人性裏以大衛後裔的身分死在十字架上時，祂將舊人連同舊造釘死在十字架上，廢除了魔鬼，在肉體中定罪了罪，並審判了世界—羅六6 ，來二14 ，羅八3 ，林後
    b. When Christ died on the cross as the seed of David in His humanity, He crucified the old man with the old creation, destroyed the devil, condemned sin in the flesh, and judged the world—Rom. 6:6; Heb. 2:14; Rom. 8:3; 2 Cor. 5:21; John 3:14; 12:31.
  7. 在復活裏 ，他的人性成為神聖的，得以『子化』，意思是說，祂不僅在祂的神性裏是神的兒子，也在他的人性裏成為神的兒子—羅一3～4：
    2. In resurrection His humanity was deified, “sonized,” meaning that He became the Son of God not only in His divinity but also in His humanity—Rom. 1:3-4:
  8. 在復活裏他被標出為神的兒子，成為神的長子，兼有神性和人性一八29。
    a. In resurrection He was designated the Son of God, made the firstborn Son of God, possessing both divinity and humanity—8:29.
  9. 釘十字架乃是使祂被標出、得榮耀並復活最好的路：(一) 一粒種子若埋在土裏死了，至終就會發芽、生長並開花，因為種子生命的作用在種子死的時候就同時得以發動—約十二23～24。
    b. Crucifixion was the best way for Him to be designated, glorified, resurrected: (1) If a seed dies by being buried in the soil, it will eventually sprout, grow, and blossom, because the operation of the seed’s life is activated simultaneously with its death—

肆、 『大衛的後裔成了神的兒子』說出我們藉著復活，被標出為神眾子的過程—來二 10~11 :

IV. The seed of David becoming the Son of God speaks of the process of our being designated the many sons of God by resurrection—Heb. 2:10-11:

一、 基督已經被標出為神的兒子，但我們仍在被標出的過程中，就是在得以子化，被作成神的過程中—羅八28~29 。

A. Christ has already been designated the Son of God, but we are still in the process of designation, the process of being sonized, deified—Rom. 8:28-29.

二、 神兒子的生命已經栽種到我們的靈裏—10 節：

B. The life of the Son of God has been implanted into our spirit—v. 10:
  1. 我們現今就像種在土裏的種子，必須經過死而復活的過程—約十二24~25 。
    1. Now we, like the seed that is sown into the earth, must pass through the process of death and resurrection—John 12:24-25.
  2. 這使外面的人被銷毀，使內裏的生命得以長大、發展，至終開花；這就是復活—林前十五31，36，林後四
    2. This causes the outer man to be consumed, but it enables the inner life to grow, to develop, and ultimately, to blossom; this is resurrection—1 Cor. 15:31, 36; 2 Cor. 4:10-12, 16.
  3. ~12 ，16。
    11. For we who are alive are always being delivered unto death for Jesus’ sake that the life of Jesus also may be manifested in our mortal ﬂesh. 12So then death operates in us, but life in you.
  4. 我們越在生命裏長大，使我們在生命裏被變化，就越被標出為神的兒子：
    3. The more we grow in life for our transformation in life, the more we are designated the sons of God:
  5. 我們要長大，就需要有轉向主的心，有純一向著祂的心—三16，18，太
    a. In order to grow, we need a heart turned to the Lord and a heart that is pure toward Him—3:16, 18; Matt. 5:8; 2 Tim. 2:22.
  6. 我們要長大，就需要有恩賜之肢體的澆灌—林前三6 下，約七 37~39 ，箴十一25。
    b. In order to grow, we need to feed on the guileless milk and the solid food of the word—1 Pet. 2:2; Heb. 5:12-14.
  7. 藉著我們環境中一切的事故,並我們的失敗,我們醜陋的己就被拆毀,使主有更多的機會在我們裏面作工—羅八28~29。
    c. In order to grow, we need the watering of the gifted members—1 Cor. 3:6b; John 7:37-39; Prov. 11:25.
  8. 有一天這個過程要完成,我們在靈、魂、體裏都要永遠與神的長子基督
    d. Through all the things in our environment and by our failures, our ugly self is torn down, and the Lord has a greater opportunity to work within us—Rom. 8:28-29.
  9. 我們被標出、得以子化、被作成神的過程,乃是復活的過程,有四個主要的方面—聖別、變化、模成和得榮—
    1. The process of our being designated, sonized, deified, is the process of resurrection with four main aspects— sanctification, transformation, conformation, and glorification—6:22; 12:2; 8:29-30.
  10. 被標出之過程的關鍵是復活,就是內住的基督作為我們靈裏興起的靈,標出的靈,生命的能力—約十一25,羅
    2. The key to the process of designation is resurrection, which is the indwelling Christ as the rising-up Spirit, the designating Spirit, the power of life in our spirit—John 11:25; Rom. 8:10-11; Acts 2:24; 1 Cor. 15:36; 5:4:
  11. Cor. 15:36—Foolish man, what you sow is not made alive unless
    11. And if the Spirit of the One who raised Jesus from the dead dwells in you, He who raised Christ from the dead will also give life to your mortal bodies through His Spirit who indwells you.
  12. t dies;
    b. The more we touch the Spirit, the more we are sanctified, transformed, and glorified to become God in life and in nature but not in the Godhead for the building up of the Body of Christ to consummate the New Jerusalem—1 Cor. 12:3; Rom. 10:12; 8:15-16; Gal. 4:6.

伍、 神的旨意是要產生祂長子的許多弟兄，使祂得著彰顯：

V. God’s purpose is to produce many brothers of His ﬁrstborn Son for His expression:

一、 羅馬八章二十九節告訴我們，神豫定我們模成神兒子的形像，使祂兒子『在許多弟兄中作長子』。

A. Romans 8:29 tells us that God predestinated us to be conformed to the image of His Son, that He may be “the Firstborn among many brothers.”

二、 長子有許多弟兄，這是奇妙的事實—太二八10，約二十17。

B. It is a wonderful fact that the Firstborn has many brothers—

三、 基督是長子，具有神性和人性；我們是祂的許多弟兄，也具有人性和神性；長子和許多兒子都在復活裏由同一位父神所生。

C. Christ is the Firstborn with divinity and humanity, and we are His many brothers with humanity and divinity; both the Firstborn and the many sons are born of the same Father God in resurrection.

四、 希伯來二章十一節，『那聖別人的，和那些被聖別的，都是出於一；因這緣故，祂稱他們為弟兄，並不以為耶。』

D. Hebrews 2:11 says, “He who sanctifies and those who are being sanctified are all of One, for which cause He is not ashamed to call them brothers.”

五、 長子是原型；我們是大量生產；基督是模型、模子、榜樣，神已將我們放在祂裏面，使我們被模成祂的形像。

E. The Firstborn is the prototype, and we are the mass production; Christ is the model, the mold, the pattern, and God has put us into Him so that we may be molded into His image.

六、 我們作為祂的許多弟兄，現今是在成熟的過程中，就是在模成祂的形像。

F. As His many brothers, we are now in the process of maturity, being conformed to His image.

七、 希伯來二章十二節引用詩篇二十二你的名，在召會中我要歌頌你。』

G. Hebrews 2:12, a quotation from Psalm 22:22, says, “I will declare Your name to My brothers; in the midst of the church I will sing hymns of praise to You.”

八、 照著希伯來二章十二節，召會是神長子的弟兄團體的組成，有兩種性情和兩種生命結合並調和在一起。

H. According to Hebrews 2:12, the church is a corporate composition of the brothers of the firstborn Son of God with two natures and two lives combined and mingled.
