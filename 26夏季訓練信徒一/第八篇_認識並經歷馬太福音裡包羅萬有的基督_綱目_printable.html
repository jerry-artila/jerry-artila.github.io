二〇二六年六月半年度訓練

第八篇 認識並經歷馬太福音裡包羅萬有的基督

Message Eight: Knowing and Experiencing the All-inclusive Christ in Matthew

讀經：太二 1~2，三 11，四 12~16，八 20，十六 16，二一 5

壹、 基督是屬天的王—太二1~2 ，二

I. Christ is the heavenly King—Matt. 2:1-2; 21:5:

一、 5：

A. Matthew proves that Jesus Christ is the King, the Messiah prophesied in the Old Testament—1:1, 17; 2:1-2; 27:11, 37.

一、 馬太福音證明耶穌是君王，是舊約所豫言的彌賽亞——1 ，17 ，二1~2 ，

B. The heavenly King did not come with haughty splendor but with gentle, humble meekness—21:5.

二七、 11，37。

A. The Christ refers to the anointed One of God and speaks of the Lord’s commission to accomplish God’s eternal purpose through His crucifixion, resurrection, ascension, and second coming—vv. 21, 27.

四、 代；從大衛到遷徙巴比倫，也是十

B. The Son of the living God speaks of His person, which embodies the Father and consummates in the Spirit for a full expression of the Triune God—John 14:10-11a; 1 Cor. 15:45b.

四、 代；從遷徙巴比倫到基督，又是十

A. Christ is the man who brings God’s dominion to earth and makes God’s name excellent on earth—9:6; 12:8; 13:41; 16:27-28.

四、 代。

B. In order for the kingdom of the heavens to be established, the Lord Jesus stood as a victorious man—a man who could defeat Satan and withstand any hardship, opposition, or attack—4:4; 12:40; 26:64.

二、 這位屬天的王並不是高傲顯赫的來，乃是溫和、謙遜、溫柔的來——二一

A. The Lord’s baptism in the Holy Spirit, which is based on His redemption, initiated the kingdom of the heavens, bringing His believers into the kingdom of the heavens—v. 12a.

一、 『基督』意指神的受膏者，是到主的使命，要藉著他的死、復活、升天和再臨，完成神永遠的定旨——21 ，27節。

B. The Lord’s baptism in fire, which is based on His judgment, will conclude the kingdom of the heavens, putting the unbelievers into the lake of fire—v. 12b.

二、 『活神的兒子』是到主的身位，具體的表現父，並終結於那靈，使三一神得著完滿的彰顯——約十四10~11上，林前十五45 下。

A. Christ’s ministry for the kingdom of the heavens began not with earthly power but with heavenly light.

參、 基督是人子——太八20，十一19，十三37，十六13：

VI. In calling people to follow Him for the kingdom, Christ ministered as a Physician and revealed Himself as the Bridegroom—9:9-15:

一、 基督就是那把神的管治權帶到地上，並使神的名在地上極其尊大的人一

B. We need to appreciate Him as the Bridegroom that we might have the enjoyment of living in His presence—vv. 14-15.

九、 6 ，十二8 ，十三41 ，十六 27~28 。

A. From His incarnation to His crucifixion, He was the unfulled cloth for making a new garment.

二、 為著諸天之國的建立，主耶穌站在得勝之人的地位上一這人能擊敗撒但並抵擋任何艱難、反對和攻撃一

B. Through His death and resurrection Christ was made a new garment to cover us as our righteousness before God that we might be justified by God and be acceptable to Him— 15:22; Gal. 3:27; 1 Cor. 1:30.

四、 4，十二40，二六64。

A. The new wine signifies the individual Christ as the new life, full of vigor and cheering strength, stirring us to excitement and satisfying us.

肆、 基督是施浸者一三11：

IX. Christ is the Shepherd and the Lord of the harvest—Matt. 9:35-38:

一、 主在聖靈裏的浸，基於祂的救贖 ，引進了諸天的國，把信的人帶進諸天的國裏一12 節上。

A. In His ministry for the establishing of His heavenly kingdom, the Lord Jesus ministered as a Shepherd—vv. 35-36.

二、 主在火裏的浸，基於祂的審判，要結束諸天的國，把不信的人丟在火湖裏一12 節下。

B. If we see the vision of Christ as the Lord of the harvest, we will beseech Him to thrust out workers into His harvest— vv. 37-38.

伍、 基督是生命的光，照在死亡的黑暗中一四12～16：

X. Christ is the Friend of sinners and the wisdom of God— 11:19: Matt. 11:19—The Son of Man came eating and drinking; and they say, Behold, a gluttonous man and a drunkard, a friend of tax collectors and sinners. Yet wisdom is justiﬁed by her works.

一、 基督為著諸天之國的職事，不是憑屬地的權能，乃是憑屬天的大光開始。

A. As the Friend of sinners, Christ sympathizes with their problems and senses their grief—v. 19a.

二、 主耶穌不是開始一個運動或革命，乃是作為大光吸引門徒歸他自己，以建立諸天的國。

B. Whatever Christ did was done by the wisdom of God, which is Himself; this wisdom was vindicated by His wise works—v. 19b; 1 Cor. 1:24, 30.

陸、 基督在為著國度呼召人跟從祂的事上，是盡職作醫生，並啟示他自己是新郎一九9～15：

XI. Christ is the One who gives rest—Matt. 11:28-30:

一、 同坐席。

A. To take the Lord’s yoke is to take the will of the Father and to be constrained by the will of the Father—v. 29; John 4:34; 5:30; 6:38.

一、 他來作醫生，要醫治並點活我們，使我們能重新構成為諸天之國的公民—9～13 節。

B. Because the Lord was always satisfied with the Father’s will, He always had rest in His heart; now He asks us to learn from Him—Matt. 11:28-30.

二、 我們需要珍賞祂是新郎，好享受與祂同在的生活—14～15 節。

A. Jonah is a type of Christ in His death, burial, and resurrection—12:39-41.

一、 成為肉體到十字架，是未漂過的布，為著作成新衣服。

B. For the evil and adulterous Jewish and religious generation, the Lord Jesus would do nothing but die and be resurrected as the greatest sign to them that they might be saved if they would believe—16:4. XIII. Christ is the bread and crumbs under the table— 15:21-38:

二、 基督藉著死而復活，就作成了一件新衣服，在神面前作我們的義，遮蓋我們，使我們能得神稱義，蒙祂悅納—十五22，加三27，林前一30。

A. God’s economy is not a matter of outward things but of Christ coming into us as food—vv. 26, 34, 36.

一、 新酒象徵個人的基督是新生命，滿有活力和激人的力量，使我們振奮 ，使我們滿足。

B. We need to take in the edible Christ by eating Him as bread, even as the crumbs under the table—v. 27. XIV. Christ in His humanity is the resurrected One with all authority in heaven and on earth—28:18- 19:

二、 新皮袋象徵團體的基督，是外面的容器，盛裝著新酒—林前十二12。

A. In His humanity, as the Son of Man and the heavenly King, all authority was given to Christ after His resurrection—v. 18.

一、 個身體，基督也是這樣。

B. Because the Gospel of Matthew is concerned with the kingdom, and the kingdom requires authority, in Matthew Christ’s resurrection is a matter of authority for discipling the nations—v. 19. XV. The Gospel of Matthew is full of the riches of Christ; we need to worship Him, enjoy Him, and experience Him as the wonderful, all-inclusive One—2:11; 28:9, 17; 17:5.
