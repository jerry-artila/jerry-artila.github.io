二〇二六年六月半年度訓練

第二篇 永遠的生命—神的生命帶著神聖的性情；三一神乃是聖經的結構；以及隱藏的神，自隱的神

Message Two: The Eternal Life—the Life of God with the Divine Nature, the Triune God Being the Structure of the Bible, and the Hidden God, a God Who Hides Himself

讀經：約四 24，彼後一 1，4～11，約壹一 5，四 8，16，路十八 1～18

壹、 神聖的生命可視為神首要且基本的屬性：

I. The divine life may be considered as the ﬁrst and the basic attribute of God:

一、 神的生命是惟一的生命。

A. God’s life is the unique life.

二、 以弗所四章十八節是說到神的生命惟一的經文：「他們在悟性上既然昏暗，就因著那在他們裏面的無知，因著他們心裏的剛硬，與神的生命隔絕了。」

B. Ephesians 4:18 is the unique verse that speaks of the life of God: “Being darkened in their understanding, alienated from the life of God because of the ignorance which is in them, because of the hardness of their heart.”

三、 在全宇宙中，惟有神的生命纔算為生命—約壹五12。

C. In the whole universe only the life of God can be counted as life—1 John 5:12.

四、 在神看來，惟有他的生命是生命；因此，新約裏題到神的生命時，就把這生命看作惟一的生命—約一4，十

D. In the sight of God only His life is life; therefore, when the life of God is mentioned in the New Testament, it is treated as if it is the unique life—John 1:4; 10:10; 11:25; 14:6.

五、 神的生命是非受造、無始無終、自有永有、永存不變的。

E. The life of God is uncreated, without beginning or ending, existing by itself, and ever, unchangeably existing.

六、 從永遠的觀點看，惟有神的生命纔是生命。

F. From the standpoint of eternity, only the life of God is life.

貳、 永遠的生命乃是神的生命帶著神聖的性情：

II. The eternal life is the life of God with the divine nature:

  4. 永遠的生命是神白白賜給的—羅六23，約壹五11。
    c. Eternal life being given freely by God—Rom. 6:23; 1 John 5:11.
  5. 永遠的生命是一信就得著的—約三16，36，五24。

二、 神的生命具有神聖的性情:

B. The life of God with the divine nature:
  1. 我們這些在基督裏的信徒既接受同樣寶貴的信,就應當有分於神的性情—彼後一4:
    1. As those who have received equally precious faith, we, the believers in Christ, should be partakers of the divine nature—2 Pet. 1:4:
  2. 神聖的性情』是指神的所是,也就是神所是的豐富、元素和構成成分—約四24,約壹一5,四8,16。
    a. The divine nature refers to what God is, that is, the riches, the elements, and the constituents of God’s being—John 4:24; 1 John 1:5; 4:8, 16.
  3. 神聖的生命和神聖的性情是分不開的;神聖的性情是神聖生命的本質,並且在神聖生命裏面—一1~2,五
